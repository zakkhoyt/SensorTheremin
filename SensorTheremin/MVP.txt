
v0.1


TouchScreen X and Y drive an instrument. (Oscillator, or other)

That instrument is put though an effects pedal which is controlled by the accelerometer. (Distortion, reverb, or similar)

The audio is visually rendered using spritekit.
The audio is rendered to the iPhone's speaker/headphones/AirPlay
